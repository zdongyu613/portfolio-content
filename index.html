Reol

Profile

Born November 9, 1993. A singer-songwriter and a multi-creator who self-produces all of his artist activities.

In 2018, started activities under the name of “Reol”.

After releasing the 1st full album "De facto", a large-scale tour was held in all 9 cities including Asia and it was a great success. And recorded the highest rank himself. In January 2020, the latest album "Kinjito" containing the song "HYPE MODE" used in the CM of "App Store" was released, and in July of the same year, YouTube conducted a global campaign "Artist on the Rise". Selected as a Japanese female artist for the first time. The boat race CM song "Sixth Sense" was released on July 29th. On August 16th, the first non-audience live "Reol Japan Tour 2020 Hameln's Great Command -Connection Edition-" will be broadcast live on YouTube and will be successful.

Currently, the number of YouTube subscribers has exceeded 1.4 million. The total number of views has exceeded 700 million, and it is gaining popularity both at home and abroad.
